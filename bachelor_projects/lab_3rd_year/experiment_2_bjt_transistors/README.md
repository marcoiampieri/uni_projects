### This folder contains the scripts used to analyze and visualize via _ROOT_ the data gathered during the second mandatory experiment of the _Electronics Laboratory_ course in the $3^{rd}$ year of the _Physics_ Bachelor's programme in Bologna.

### The experiment aimed to verify the correct behavior of a BJT transistor in a common-emitter configuration by measuring its characteristic I-V curve for different $I_B$ (_base current_) values.

## Content
- _**fit_100muA.cpp**_: here the data gathered with $I_B=100$ is loaded, fitted and plotted, and the statistical parameters are computed
- _**fit_200muA.cpp**_: here the data gathered with $I_B=200$ is loaded, fitted and plotted, and the statistical parameters are computed
- _**multigraph.cpp**_: here both the dataset are plotted, along with their respective fits
- _**data/**_: folder containing the data points gathered during the experiment
- _**results/**_: folder containing the results generated by the _.cpp_ files
